
Helping the robot use the elevator.

In the elevator there is a section of buttons withs numbers.
Each number represents a floor. The higher the number higher you travel.
if the level is greater than 1 you go up in numerical order up until 48.
If your level is less than 48 you go down in numerical order up to one.
Once you have reached the desired floor please exit the elevator.
